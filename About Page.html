ABOUT US

Bula Kpop is a Fijian Company, established on May 7th 2023 that makes it easier for island kpop stans to purchase their dream albums and lightsticks. Chief Excutive Officer Shaya Mizo knows the struggle you face when trying to purchase and support your idols; the outrages prices when converted to Fijian Dollars, the weeks of waiting for products to arrive and the fear of losing your money on a sketchy website. With Bula Kpop there is nothing to fear.
